Support

Ahead helps you create custom mental warm-ups for moments you want to handle better. For support, privacy, account, or subscription questions, email m20sethi@gmail.com.

Response time: we aim to respond within 2 business days.

Subscriptions: purchases, refunds, and cancellations are handled through Apple App Store subscriptions.

Data deletion: email us with the subject "Ahead data deletion" and include the anonymous support identifier shown in the app if available.

Common Questions

Is Ahead therapy? No. Ahead is not therapy, medical care, mental health treatment, or crisis support.

Can I restore a purchase? Yes. Use Restore Purchases inside the app. If it does not work, email support.

Can I delete my data? Yes. Email us and we will delete or anonymize app data associated with your support identifier where legally possible.
