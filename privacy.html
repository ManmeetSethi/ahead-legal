Ahead

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 July 2026

Ahead is an iOS app that creates custom mental warm-ups for moments you want to handle better, such as interviews, difficult conversations, avoidance, and performance pressure. Ahead is anonymous-first: you can use it without giving us your name or email. Ahead is operated by Manmeet Sethi as an individual developer. For privacy questions or data requests, email m20sethi@gmail.com.

2. How We Use Data

  • Provide the core product: custom generated mental warm-ups and playback.
  • Personalize sessions based on your selected category, situation, feedback, and instructions.
  • Evaluate generated scripts before voice generation.
  • Activate subscriptions, restore purchases, and enforce usage limits.
  • Debug problems, monitor quality, and improve product reliability.
  • Measure advertising performance and understand which campaigns lead to installs or subscriptions.

We do not sell your personal data.

3. AI Processing

The situations and instructions you provide may be sent to AI model providers so Ahead can generate a custom session and evaluate whether the output is good enough before audio is created. Please do not enter information you are not permitted to share.

5. Advertising and Meta App Events

Ahead may use the Meta SDK to measure app installs, app activation, and subscription-related events so we can understand whether our advertising is working. The Meta SDK may process device and app metadata such as app version, device type, operating system, event timestamps, and campaign attribution signals. Ahead currently disables advertiser ID collection in the app. If we later ask for App Tracking Transparency permission or collect advertising identifiers, we will update the app experience and this policy accordingly.

6. Payment Data

Payments are processed by Apple and RevenueCat. Ahead does not receive or store your card or bank details. We store only the purchase and entitlement information needed to activate access, enforce usage limits, and provide support.

7. Retention

We keep app data while needed to provide Ahead, support your subscription, debug the service, and meet legal, tax, fraud-prevention, accounting, or security obligations. Generated audio and logs may be deleted periodically as the product matures.

8. Your Choices

  • You can request deletion of your Ahead data by emailing m20sethi@gmail.com.
  • You can manage subscriptions through Apple App Store subscription settings.
  • You can turn off notifications in iOS Settings.
  • You can limit ad tracking using iOS privacy settings. Ahead does not currently ask for App Tracking Transparency permission.

9. Children's Privacy

Ahead is not directed to children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Safety Note

Ahead is not therapy, medical care, mental health treatment, diagnosis, or crisis support. If you are in danger or need urgent help, contact local emergency services or a qualified professional.

11. Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, email m20sethi@gmail.com.
